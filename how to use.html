For Tracking The Buses

Step 1 : Click on track a bus icon or link.

Step 2 : You redirected on another page that is track buses page.

Step 3 : Then choose departure from the drop down options.

Step 4 : Then after choose destination from the drop down options.

Step 5 : Then select bus type either government or private.

Step 6 : Then select the journey date & time.

Step 7 : After filling the above details, click on Track button.


For Giving The Information

Step 1 : Click on give bus info icon or link.

Step 2 : You redirected on another page that is give bus information.

Step 3 : Then select bus type either government or private.

Step 4 : Then choose place from where bus is coming from, choose from drop down options.

Step 5 : Then select current bus location.

Step 6 : Then after select the current date & time.

Step 7 : After filling the above details, click on Send button.
