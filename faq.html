Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Is this a government website?

A: No, this is not a government website. This project is created by UNSIET (VBSPU) students.

Q: Who created this website?

A: Students of VBSPU named Ankit Vishwakarma, Harsh Singh & Harsh Vardhan Singh.

Q: How this website works?

A: This website completly runs on the information provided by the people and conductor who are currently traveling in bus.

Q: What happend if no one give any information?

A: In that case we don't get any type of information. So, the information not get updated on the site.

Q: What if anyone try to mislead with the wrong information?

A: In that case we fetch the number of information from our data base and update only that informtion which are majority of people provided.
